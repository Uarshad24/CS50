NEW YORK

The City That Never Sleeps
  • New York City, often simply called New York, is the largest city in the United States. Located on the East Coast, it has a population of over 8 million people within its five boroughs.
  • Founded in 1624 as New Amsterdam, it grew into a global hub for finance, culture, and technology, often referred to as "The Big Apple." With its iconic skyline, Broadway, and diverse population, New York is one of the most influential cities in the world.
