LONDON

The Square Mile

  • London is the capital city of the United Kingdom and one of the most influential cities in the world. Located on the River Thames, it has a population of over 8 million people within its city limits, with the wider metropolitan area exceeding 13 million.

  • Founded by the Romans as Londinium around AD 43, London has evolved into a global center for finance, culture, and politics, known for landmarks like the Tower of London, Buckingham Palace, and the Houses of Parliament. Rich in history and diversity, London remains a dynamic, vibrant metropolis.
